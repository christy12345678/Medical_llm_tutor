# Medical Curriculum Training Data


## Chemistry (Year 1)
Subtopics: Basic Practical Chemistry, Principles of Chemistry

## Biology (Year 1)
Subtopics: Genetics for Medical Students, General Biology

## Physics (Year 1)
Subtopics: General Physics for Life Sciences, Practical Physics

## General Studies (Year 1)
Subtopics: Use of English, Library & Study Skills, Issues of Peace & Conflict

## Gross Anatomy (Year 2)
Subtopics: Skeletal system, Muscular system, Nervous system, Organ systems

## Embryology and Genetics (Year 2)
Subtopics: Embryonic development, Genetic principles, Congenital abnormalities

## Histology and Histochemistry (Year 2)
Subtopics: Tissue types, Cellular organization, Microscopy

## Biochemistry (Year 2)
Subtopics: Carbohydrate metabolism, Protein metabolism, Lipid metabolism, Enzyme kinetics

## Physiology (Year 2)
Subtopics: Blood physiology, Cardiovascular physiology, Respiratory physiology, GI physiology

## Pharmacology (Year 3)
Subtopics: General Pharmacology, Drug classification, Pharmacokinetics, Pharmacodynamics

## Microbiology (Year 3)
Subtopics: Bacteriology, Virology, Parasitology, Immunology

## Pathology (Year 3)
Subtopics: Cell injury, Inflammation, Neoplasia, Systemic pathology

## Clinical Chemistry (Year 3)
Subtopics: Lab diagnostics, Metabolic pathways, Clinical correlations

## Internal Medicine (Year 3)
Subtopics: Cardiology I, Respiratory Medicine I, Gastroenterology, Nephrology I, Neurology I, Hematology

## Clinical Surgery (Year 3)
Subtopics: General Surgery, GI Surgery, Congenital Abnormalities, Urology, Trauma Surgery

## Advanced Pharmacology (Year 4)
Subtopics: Antimicrobial drugs, CNS drugs, Chemotherapy, Special therapeutics

## Advanced Microbiology (Year 4)
Subtopics: Medical Protozoology, Medical Helminthology, Medical Entomology

## Hematology (Year 4)
Subtopics: Blood cell disorders, Hemostasis, Blood transfusion, Hematologic malignancies

## Obstetrics and Gynaecology (Year 4)
Subtopics: Reproductive physiology, Pregnancy, Labour, Gynecologic disorders

## Pediatrics (Year 4)
Subtopics: Growth and development, Pediatric diseases, Neonatal care, Pediatric nutrition

## Forensic Medicine (Year 4)
Subtopics: Medical jurisprudence, Toxicology, Death investigation

## Obstetric Clinics (Year 5)
Subtopics: Antenatal care, Labor management, Postpartum care, High-risk obstetrics

## Pediatric Clinics (Year 5)
Subtopics: Pediatric endocrinology, Pediatric neurology, Pediatric surgery, Infectious diseases

## Community Health (Year 5)
Subtopics: Public health, Epidemiology, Primary health care, Health promotion

## Advanced Internal Medicine (Year 6)
Subtopics: Cardiology II, Respiratory Medicine II, Gastroenterology II, Nephrology II, Neurology II

## Advanced Surgery (Year 6)
Subtopics: Cardiothoracic surgery, Neurosurgery, Oncologic surgery, Vascular surgery

## Tropical Medicine (Year 6)
Subtopics: Endemic diseases, Parasitic infections, Vector-borne diseases, Infectious disease management

## Medical Ethics (Year 6)
Subtopics: Professional ethics, Jurisprudence, Medical-legal issues, Patient rights
